Our Mission

Kerala Flood OpenStreetMap (KFOM) is dedicated to enhancing community resilience and safety during flood events in Kerala. Our mission is to provide a robust, real-time flood reporting and visualization platform that empowers citizens and supports effective disaster response.

Key Features

  • Real-time flood incident reporting
  • Interactive map visualization of flood-affected areas
  • Community-driven data collection and verification
  • Historical flood data analysis for better preparedness

How KFOM Helps

For Citizens:

KFOM provides a user-friendly platform for reporting and accessing up-to-date flood information. This empowers individuals to make informed decisions about their safety and helps communities coordinate local response efforts.

For Authorities:

Our platform offers a comprehensive, real-time view of flood situations across Kerala. This valuable data aids in resource allocation, evacuation planning, and overall disaster management strategies.

Join Us in Building a Resilient Kerala

Your participation in KFOM contributes to a safer, more prepared community. Together, we can mitigate the impact of floods and protect lives and livelihoods across Kerala.
